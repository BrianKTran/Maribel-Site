Break the Chain

THE HARD TRUTH

The Body of Christ need to embrace prophecy and encourage the gifts in the Body. Some churches because of experiences are skeptical but we need to encourage the gifts to operate and is okay for churches to birth other ministries as long as it is done in decentcy and in order. The expansion of the kingdom consist at times of sending others out in the mission field or to build churches and ministries in other areas as the Lord calls and appoints. If we are called into a ministry we need a covering and need to work with the Body. God does not call us apart to work by ourselves , accountability is good. The disciples were accountable to one another to Jesus and the apostles as well.

Many times a prophet is without Honor in his own company as scriptures teaches us.

Prophets are still in the land today in the 21 century.

Greater Glory -We are Carriers of Gods Glory

In John 17 Jesus prayed for the Body to the father that the same glory be in us and with us . That same glory is upon us and in us , the body of Christ. Not a title, not a few christians, not just the apostle , prophet or pastor this Glory is upon and in all beleivers and followers of Jesus Christ. Jesus is soon to come back and he is constantly leading us in all perfection in him for he is coming back for a Glorious Holy church. Stay ready, Watch and pray, stay sober be Vigilant!!! Walk in the Unity and love in the Body , love your enemies pray for those that persecute you and know that our battle is not agianst flesh and blood.
